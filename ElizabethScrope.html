Elizabeth Scrope Born about 1439 in Bolton, Lancashire, England
Died: 12 Jun 1503 Buried: Stoke Rochford, Lincolnshire,England

Elizabeth "Isabel" de St John formerly Scrope aka Bigod, Rochford. Born about 1439 in Bolton, Lancashire, England
The Scropes were an important North Yorkshire family who spent a lot of time on the borders fighting the Scots. Elizabeth married three times. Elizabeth Scope had been married firstly to John Bigod of Settrington. He died as a result of injuries sustained at the Battle of Towton in 1461. She then married Henry Rochford who died in 1470 though whether it was from natural causes or a nasty case of soldiering is unknown. When Elizabeth died in 1503 she was buried next to husband number two in Stoke Rochford. Her third marriage was to Oliver St John.

Elizabeth's brother had married Elizatbeth St John - Oliver's Sister. He was John Scrope, Baron Scrope of Bolton (Bolton Castle in Wensleydale) and another good Yorkist.
